Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations up to 30 days in advance. You can make a reservation online through our website, by phone, or in person. For parties of 8 or more, please call us directly.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer full-service catering for events of all sizes. Please contact us at catering@tastehub.com for more information about our catering menu and services.

Is there parking available?

We have a dedicated parking lot behind our restaurant with free parking for our guests. Street parking is also available, and there's a public parking garage one block away.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options on our menu. If you have specific allergies or dietary needs, please inform your server, and our chef will be happy to accommodate you.
